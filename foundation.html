Ravi Chhikara Foundation

From the Founders Desk

Welcome to the Ravi Chhikara Foundation, a beacon of hope, inspiration, and transformative change for individuals and communities in need. Founded with an unwavering commitment to fostering a more just, inclusive, and equitable society, our foundation serves as the philanthropic extension of Ravi Chhikara's vision and dedication. Together with a passionate team of advocates, professionals, and change-makers, we strive to create lasting impact in the lives of those we serve.
At the Ravi Chhikara Foundation, we believe that every individual, regardless of their background, deserves an opportunity to thrive. Our mission is rooted in uplifting the underprivileged, empowering marginalized communities, and advocating for those whose voices often go unheard. From providing access to education and healthcare to promoting sustainable development and social justice, we are dedicated to addressing systemic inequalities and building pathways to a brighter future for all.

Our Mission

Providing Pro Bono Assistance

Justice should never be a privilege; it is a right. Our team offers free legal aid to individuals and communities who cannot afford representation, ensuring that fairness and justice prevail for all.

Empowering Communities

Beyond legal services, we work tirelessly to empower marginalized communities through education, awareness campaigns, and skill development initiatives, fostering self-reliance and dignity.

Promoting Social Good

We are committed to tackling pressing social issues, from advocating for human rights to supporting environmental sustainability, gender equality, and access to education.
