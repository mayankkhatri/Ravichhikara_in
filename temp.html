Adv. Ravi Singh Chhikara

Advocate | Animal Rights Activist | Civil and Criminal Laws | Columnist

Welcome to my professional space! I am an advocate based in North Delhi, specializing in a diverse range of legal areas, including Real Estate, Criminal Law, Economic Offences, Service Law, Arbitrations, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, Labor Law, and more. My extensive experience allows me to navigate the complexities of both civil and criminal matters effectively, ensuring that my clients receive the highest standard of legal representation.

Throughout my career, I have successfully represented clients before the Hon'ble Supreme Court, the Delhi High Court, and various tribunals across Delhi, Haryana, and Punjab. My commitment to ensuring justice for all drives my work, and I take pride in advocating for my clients' rights and interests with diligence and integrity.


Beyond my legal practice, I am passionate about raising awareness on critical issues, particularly in the realm of animal rights. I believe in using my platform to advocate for those who cannot speak for themselves. Whether in the courtroom or through my written work, I strive to make a positive impact on society.
